Covid Fundraiser (2021)

Raised $700 for oxygen meters in a rural village in India

During the height of the pandemic, I organized a small art fundraiser to support families in a rural village in India who were struggling to access healthcare and basic medical supplies. I created over 50 handmade coasters and more than 20 mugs and sold them at my school during the last couple weeks of sixth grade.

Friends, family, and my school community responded to the art fundraiser with amazing generosity. Together we raised $700, which was used to purchase and distribute oxygen meters through a local community organization. I was so glad that I had been able to help people on the other side of the globe with my artwork, even if it was just one village.
